    🎬 Guided scenario — authority survives the superadmin going offline

    Alice's window stays open the whole time — she is the relay. Watch the roster and your role update even with the superadmin closed.

    1. 🛡️ Superadmin — log in, then in Govern promote Alice to manager. Alice's role updates live.
    2. 🛡️ Superadmin — close this browser. The peers count drops: the signer is now offline.
    3. 👨‍🦱 Bob — open a browser and log in. Only Alice is online; Bob is a guest.
    4. 👨‍🦱 Bob — close Bob's browser.
    5. 🛡️ Superadmin — reopen and promote Bob by address, even though Bob is offline. Alice receives & stores the signed grant.
    6. 🛡️ Superadmin — close again. Only Alice now holds Bob's promotion.
    7. 👨‍🦱 Bob — reopen. Alice (not the superadmin!) relays the grant — Bob's role rises ✨, verified by the superadmin's signature.
